About Breadom LLC

Breadom LLC is a privately owned company founded by Duke Bread, Tyrell Scott, Lucy Wheat, Win Pro, Nora Hatija, Yohanna Finch, Maria Xo, Nick , and Frank Rowe. The company makes mobile games, and are known for their mobile game UltraBeing. UltraBeing is a life simulator where the player can start a second life and be whoever they want, whenever they want.
