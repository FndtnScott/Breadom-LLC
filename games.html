Alkherm Online - 1.5.6

Description:

Alkherm Online is an open-world, dark fantasy, text-based, choice-based medieval RPG. Players can immerse themselves in a variety of roles, from battling monsters to becoming a revered Hero, or joining the dark side to lead the demon army as the fearsome Lord. Alternatively, one can live a peaceful life as a blacksmith, woodcutter, or chef.

Players can form relationships with NPCs, choosing to have children or marry without offspring, and they can climb the social ladder to become nobles or remain commoners. In this RPG, the possibilities are endless. Take up your trade and step into the rich, immersive world of Alkherm.
